# Provider-Aware Agentic Systems

Agentic AI systems in financial services need to understand more than task intent. They need to understand the data boundary.

Financial data providers differ in identifiers, taxonomies, entitlements, attribution obligations, update cadence, point-in-time behavior, restatement policies, and source lineage. These details shape what an AI system can responsibly say or do.

A provider-aware agentic system should preserve:

- provider and dataset identity
- original and normalized identifiers
- entitlement scope
- as-of and effective dates
- attribution requirements
- source lineage and transformation history
- confidence and review status

This is what separates a helpful workflow system from a plausible but ungoverned AI layer.
