# WealthTech AI Product Thesis

The most valuable AI opportunities in wealthtech are not only better dashboards or faster summaries.

The higher-value wedge is the workflow before the dashboard: turning messy evidence into reviewed financial intelligence.

Advisors work with incomplete documents, held-away assets, inconsistent statements, PDFs, screenshots, spreadsheets, notes, tax records, insurance details, private investments, business interests, and client memories. A useful AI system should help structure that evidence without pretending the evidence is cleaner than it is.

The product pattern is:

```text
messy evidence
  -> source-linked facts
  -> uncertainty and contradiction detection
  -> advisor review queue
  -> approved financial model
  -> dashboard, brief, checklist, and client story
```

The advisor stays responsible for judgment. The system makes the work faster, clearer, and easier to defend.
